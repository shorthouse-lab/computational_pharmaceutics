Context¶

Imagine you are involved in running a clinical trial measuring the effect of a new drug in three different treatment groups: Placebo, Low Dose, and High Dose on patients’ cholesterol levels.

For each patient, you have recorded:

  • SubjectID: Unique participant identifier
  • TreatmentGroup: Placebo, LowDose, or HighDose
  • Age: Patient’s age
  • Gender: M or F
  • BMI: Body Mass Index
  • CholesterolPre: Patient’s cholesterol level before the trial
  • CholesterolPost: Patient’s cholesterol level after the trial
  • SideEffectsScale: A subjective scale (1-10) indicating the severity of side effects, with 1 = no side effects and 10 = severe side effects

The data is available in an excel file called "clinical_trial_results.xlsx" which is available in the github repository.

The overall goal of the trial is to see if the drugs lower cholesterol in patients, and whether there are any significant side effects.

from scipy import stats

# Group the patients into each treatment group and get their cholesterol levels
placebo_chol = df_clean.loc[df_clean["TreatmentGroup"] == "Placebo", "CholesterolPost"]
lowdose_chol = df_clean.loc[df_clean["TreatmentGroup"] == "LowDose", "CholesterolPost"]
highdose_chol = df_clean.loc[df_clean["TreatmentGroup"] == "HighDose", "CholesterolPost"]

# ANOVA across all three treatment groups
f_stat, p_val = stats.f_oneway(placebo_chol, lowdose_chol, highdose_chol)
print("\nANOVA for CholesterolPost:")
print("F-Statistic:", f_stat, "   P-value:", p_val)

Question 9¶

In this study - patients who were on one of three different treatment regimes were compared for the difference between their post and pre-treatment cholesterol levels. Patients in the different treatment groups had different precholesterol levels (ANOVA p-value = 9.5e-10), with a posthoc test determining all differences were significant, with the high dose treatment group having the lowest levels post-treatment. There were no significant correlations between age and cholesterol levels either pre or post treatment. Caution should be taken when continuing with the drug in future studies however - as the side effects were seen to increase between the groups, with the high treatment group having a mean side effect severity of 5.5 out of 10.
